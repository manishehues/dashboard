In Salon Consulation
  • Overview
  • Options

Durations and prices set at the business may be overridden at each location, for each staff role, or for each staff member individually.

Processing Time :A block of time at the end of the service when a client is busy, but the staff can attend other clients. Generally used for hair color processing.

Finishing Time :A block of time at the end of a service and any processing or transition time when both the client and the staff are busy.

Transition Time : A block of time after the service is performed during which the staff member is unavailable. Use this for cleanup, buffer, documentation time, break time, etc.
